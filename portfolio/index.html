InsightAI

AI-Powered Business Intelligence Platform

Project Overview

InsightAI is an AI-powered Business Intelligence platform that transforms raw CSV and Excel datasets into actionable business insights. The platform automates data cleaning, validation, KPI generation, forecasting, interactive dashboards, and provides a dataset-aware AI assistant for natural language business queries.

Problem Statement

Organizations often struggle to convert raw business data into meaningful insights due to poor data quality, manual analysis, and lack of analytical expertise. InsightAI simplifies this process by automating data preparation, visualization, forecasting, and business intelligence reporting.

Objectives

  • Automate dataset cleaning and validation.
  • Generate interactive dashboards and KPI analytics.
  • Predict future sales trends using Machine Learning.
  • Develop a dataset-aware AI assistant for business queries.
  • Generate executive reports in PDF and Excel formats.

Key Features

  • Dataset Upload: Supports CSV, XLS and XLSX files.
  • Automated Data Cleaning: Missing value handling, duplicate removal, outlier detection and quality scoring.
  • Interactive Dashboard: KPI cards, sales trends, customer insights and regional analytics.
  • Sales Forecasting: Linear Regression model for predicting future business trends.
  • AI Assistant: Dataset-aware assistant capable of answering business questions using real-time Pandas analysis.
  • Executive Reports: Export business reports in PDF and Excel formats.
  • Secure Authentication: User login, registration and role-based access.

Technology Stack

Python Flask Pandas NumPy Scikit-learn SQLite HTML CSS JavaScript Plotly

Challenges & Solutions

  • Data Quality: Built an automated pipeline for missing value handling, duplicate removal, type conversion, and outlier detection.
  • Natural Language Queries: Developed a dataset-aware AI assistant using intent detection and dynamic Pandas-based analytics.
  • Business Forecasting: Implemented Linear Regression to generate short-term sales predictions and business insights.

Future Scope

  • Support advanced forecasting models such as ARIMA and Prophet.
  • Cloud deployment using AWS or Microsoft Azure.
  • Role-based dashboards for multiple business departments.
  • Semantic AI assistant using Large Language Models and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
  • Real-time anomaly detection and intelligent business alerts.

Lessons Learned
